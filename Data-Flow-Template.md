## Objective

To document how routine clinical information moves through healthcare facilities and to identify points where data becomes lost, delayed, duplicated, or unavailable for secondary use, including:

* Public health reporting
* Epidemiological surveillance
* Health system planning
* AI and data-driven health analytics

This template is designed to understand the operational pathways that determine whether routine clinical information becomes structured, reusable data.
